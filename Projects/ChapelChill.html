Chapel Chill

For Chapel Chill, I designed a high-fidelity prototype for the Chapel Chill app, focusing on a main page displaying nearby social events, an event details screen, and a personal events page that I added to enhance the app's functionality. The design incorporated a vibrant yet clean aesthetic, reflecting the app's goal of promoting a fun and safe environment for college students, while adhering to Apple's Human Interface Guidelines to ensure a seamless user experience.

Overview

For the Chapel Chill project, I was responsible for developing an in-depth Figma prototype of an app designed to help students discover and track campus events. My focus was on creating an intuitive, user-friendly experience that reflected the app’s friendly and outgoing vibe. I incorporated key features like a personalized event calendar that allows users to bookmark events and receive reminders, ensuring they don’t miss out on activities that interest them. The prototype also emphasized clear navigation and a visually appealing design, making the app feel welcoming while helping students stay connected and engaged with campus life.
